## Orchestration contract

- The default coding selector may choose only dependency-satisfied incomplete code items; external evidence and human approval never become coding-agent work.
- One integrator owns main, central registries, canonical artifacts, commits, and pushes; reviewers remain read-only while a slice is active.
- A product-facing capability closes only with its listed acceptance evidence and explicit proof boundary; additional wrappers or commits are not progress by themselves.
- Local, fixture, and hosted-like evidence may prepare operator handoffs but cannot complete an external-evidence item.
- Optional work remains outside required completion until its decision trigger fires.
